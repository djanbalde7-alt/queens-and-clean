Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 31, 2025

At Queens & Clean, we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the rights and choices you have under applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA).

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact & Booking Data: Full name, email, phone number, address, preferred date, service type, number of bedrooms, and other booking preferences.
  • Communication Data: Messages you send through our forms, chat, email, or SMS.
  • Transactional Data: Payment confirmations and receipts (we do not store full card numbers).
  • Usage Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and pages visited on our site.
  • Cookies & Analytics: Small files stored on your device to improve site performance, analytics, and advertising preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, schedule, and manage cleaning services and quotes.
  • To respond to inquiries and offer customer support.
  • To send transactional and service-related emails (via SendGrid).
  • To analyze site usage and improve performance and user experience.
  • To send marketing communications, only with your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations and maintain business records.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or by using our cookie consent banner.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site operation (e.g., submitting forms, navigation).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior to improve site performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used by HubSpot or Meta to measure and optimize ad performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences such as language and consent choices.

5. Service Providers and Data Processors

  • HubSpot (CRM & Forms): We use HubSpot to collect and manage leads, forms, and service bookings. Read HubSpot’s Privacy Policy.
  • SendGrid (Email Delivery): Used to send transactional and confirmation emails. SendGrid Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify (Hosting): Our website is securely hosted on Netlify servers. Netlify Privacy Policy.

6. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Performance of a contract (when you request or book a service).
  • Legitimate interests (to manage customer relationships, prevent fraud, improve quality).
  • Consent (for marketing emails and analytics cookies).
  • Legal obligations (to comply with tax, accounting, and data protection laws).

7. Data Retention Periods

  • Customer & Booking Data: kept for up to 3 years after your last service.
  • Marketing Data: retained until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.
  • Email logs (SendGrid): 90 days for delivery verification.
  • Analytics Data: anonymized or deleted after 26 months.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also:

  • Withdraw your consent for marketing at any time.
  • Opt out of cookies or analytics.
  • Request a copy of your data (email: hello@queensandclean.com).

9. California Residents (CPRA)

California residents have the right to request access, deletion, or correction of their personal data, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise this right, visit our Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page.

10. Security Measures

We use SSL encryption and other safeguards to protect your personal data. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect new features, technologies, or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
