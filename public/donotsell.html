Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Effective date: October 31, 2025

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have the right to request that their personal information not be “sold” or “shared” for purposes of targeted or cross-context behavioral advertising.

1. Our Policy

Queens & Clean does not sell personal information for monetary value. However, we may share limited customer data (e.g., device identifiers or page visits) with trusted partners like analytics and advertising tools to improve user experience or measure marketing performance. Under California law, this may be considered “sharing.”

You can opt out of this sharing anytime by submitting a request below or managing your cookie preferences directly through our cookie banner.

2. What “Personal Information” May Be Shared

  • Identifiers (e.g., IP address, device ID, cookies)
  • Internet or network activity (e.g., pages viewed, engagement time)
  • Inferred preferences used for marketing segmentation

3. How to Submit a Request

To exercise your “Do Not Sell or Share” rights, please complete the form below. We will confirm receipt and process your request within 45 days.

You can also email us directly at hello@queensandclean.com with the subject line “CPRA Opt-Out”.

4. Verification Process

To protect your privacy, we may verify your identity before processing your request. Verification may include confirming your email address or information previously provided when booking or contacting us.

5. Cookie & Advertising Preferences

You can manage cookie-based sharing by adjusting your preferences in our cookie consent banner or through your browser’s “Do Not Track” settings.

6. Authorized Agents

You may authorize another person to submit a request on your behalf. To do so, please provide written authorization signed by you and your agent.
